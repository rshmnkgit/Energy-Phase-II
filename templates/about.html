Description:

Renewable energy is energy that comes from natural resources such as sunlight, wind, rain, tides, plants, waves, and geothermal heat. These energy resources are renewable, meaning they are naturally replenished. The major types of renewable energy sources: Biomass, Hydropower, Wind and Solar.


Questions:
  • Usage of Renewable Energies all over the World
  • How are the relations between GDP of each countries and Renewable Energies Consumption and Generation?
  • Display the effect of the Renewable Energies Generation on Air Pollution and Death Rate by years

    • ETL Process:
  • Cleaning of the data using Pandas and Jupyter Notebook.
  • Transformed the data into dictionaries using Python.
  • Webscraping with chromedriver version 86 to extract definitons, advantages and disadvatgaes of different types of renewable energy.
  • Transformed the scraped data into dictionary.
  • Loaded the trasformed data into Mongo Cloud database.

    • Application development and deployment:
  • Used the Python as the server-side language, Flask as the web server and Mongo Cloud as the ORM.
  • Used HTML, CSS and JavaScript on the front end.
  • Used D3, Plotly, Chart.js, Leaflet and Mapbox to visualize the data.
  • Deployed the web application using Heroku

    • Analysis:

    In this project, we analyzed 3 key points;
    1) We found that the renewable energy generation is increasing in most of the countries during the past 20 years in the World.
    2) We found that the death rate due to air pollution is decreasing as the renewable enegry production is increasing.
    3) We observed how the GDP per capita of each country would change the generation and consumption of Renewable Energy.
